Our Story

About Christora Ceylon

Christora Ceylon (Pvt) Ltd is a trusted exporter of premium natural products from the heart of Sri Lanka, dedicated to bringing the authentic flavors and natural goodness of the island to customers around the world.

As a growing company with a passion for quality and excellence, we carefully source and deliver products that reflect Sri Lanka’s rich agricultural heritage. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and ethical business practices ensures that every product meets high international standards. By working closely with local farmers and trusted producers, we guarantee authenticity while supporting the communities behind Sri Lanka’s finest natural products.
